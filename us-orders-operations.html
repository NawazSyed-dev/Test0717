Orders and Operations

Comprehensive guide for managing POS hardware orders and operational procedures

Order Management

How do I place a new POS hardware order? +

To place a new POS hardware order:

  • Access the ordering portal through your merchant dashboard
  • Select hardware type (Ingenico, Verifone, or other supported models)
  • Specify quantity and delivery location details
  • Choose deployment timeline and any special requirements
  • Submit order and receive confirmation with tracking information

Orders are typically processed within 24-48 hours and you'll receive email updates throughout the process.

What information is required for bulk orders? +

For bulk orders (10+ terminals), please provide:

  • Site survey details including number of locations
  • Network infrastructure specifications at each site
  • Deployment schedule with preferred installation dates
  • Technical contact information for each location
  • Special requirements such as mounting preferences or custom configurations

Bulk orders receive priority processing and dedicated project management support.

How can I modify or cancel an existing order? +

Order modifications depend on the current status:

  • Pending orders can be modified or cancelled without fees
  • Processing orders may incur modification fees
  • Shipped orders require return authorization for changes

Contact our support team immediately at 1-800-POS-HELP for urgent modifications.

Operational Procedures

What is the standard deployment timeline? +

Standard deployment timeline:

  • Order processing: 1-2 business days
  • Hardware preparation: 2-3 business days
  • Shipping: 3-5 business days (standard) or 1-2 days (expedited)
  • Installation scheduling: Within 5 business days of delivery
  • Configuration and testing: 1-2 hours on-site

Total timeline: 7-12 business days from order to go-live.

What happens during the installation process? +

Our certified technicians will:

  • Unpack and inspect all hardware components
  • Configure network settings and establish secure connections
  • Install payment applications and configure merchant settings
  • Test all payment methods including chip, contactless, and PIN
  • Train your staff on basic operations and troubleshooting
  • Provide documentation and emergency contact information
How do I handle emergency hardware failures? +

For emergency hardware failures:

  • Call our 24/7 emergency line: 1-800-POS-911
  • Provide your merchant ID and describe the issue
  • Follow remote troubleshooting steps provided by our technician
  • If unresolvable, emergency replacement hardware will be dispatched
  • Same-day replacement available in major metropolitan areas

Emergency replacements are typically delivered within 4-6 hours during business hours.

Quality Assurance

What quality checks are performed before deployment? +

Every terminal undergoes comprehensive testing:

  • Hardware diagnostics including screen, keypad, and connectivity tests
  • Software validation ensuring latest firmware and security patches
  • Payment processing tests with test cards for all supported methods
  • Network security verification including encryption and certificate validation
  • Compliance certification for PCI DSS and EMV standards

All tests are documented and included with your deployment package.
