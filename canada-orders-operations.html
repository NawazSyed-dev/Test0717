Comprehensive guide for managing POS hardware orders and operational procedures in Canada

Order Management

How do I place a new POS hardware order in Canada? +

To place a new POS hardware order in Canada:

  • Access the Canadian ordering portal through your merchant dashboard
  • Select hardware type (Ingenico, Verifone, or other Health Canada approved models)
  • Specify quantity and province for proper tax calculation
  • Choose bilingual configuration (English/French) if required
  • Submit order and receive confirmation with Canada Post tracking

Orders are processed within 24-48 hours with email updates in English and French.

What are the provincial tax considerations for orders? +

Provincial tax requirements vary by location:

  • HST provinces (ON, NB, NS, NL, PE): 13-15% HST applies
  • GST + PST provinces (BC, SK, MB, QC): 5% GST + provincial PST
  • GST only (AB, NT, NU, YT): 5% GST only
  • Tax exemptions available for registered charities and government entities

All pricing includes applicable taxes and duties for Canadian delivery.

How do cross-border shipments work? +

For cross-border shipments:

  • Customs documentation is handled automatically
  • Duty and brokerage fees are pre-calculated and included
  • CBSA clearance typically adds 1-2 business days
  • Tracking updates provided through customs process

All hardware meets Industry Canada certification requirements.

Canadian Compliance

What are the bilingual requirements for POS terminals? +

Bilingual requirements depend on your location:

  • Quebec: French language display required by law
  • New Brunswick: Bilingual options recommended
  • Federal institutions: Must support both official languages
  • Other provinces: English default with French option available

All terminals can be configured for bilingual operation during setup.

How do I configure Interac debit processing? +

Interac debit configuration includes:

  • Interac certification verification for your merchant account
  • Network routing setup for Interac transaction processing
  • PIN verification and transaction limit configuration
  • Contactless Interac Flash setup for tap payments
  • Testing procedures with Interac test cards

Our technicians handle all Interac-specific configurations during installation.

Support and Maintenance

What are the support hours for Canadian customers? +

Canadian support coverage:

  • 24/7 Emergency Line: 1-800-POS-HELP (available in English and French)
  • Business Hours: 6 AM - 10 PM EST (covers all time zones)
  • French Support: Available during Quebec business hours
  • Holiday Coverage: Includes Canadian statutory holidays

Emergency replacement hardware available in major Canadian cities within 4-6 hours.

How do warranty claims work in Canada? +

Canadian warranty process:

  • Standard warranty: 3 years parts and labor
  • Extended warranty: Available up to 5 years
  • Return shipping: Prepaid Canada Post labels provided
  • Replacement timeline: 3-5 business days within Canada
  • Consumer protection: Complies with provincial consumer protection laws
